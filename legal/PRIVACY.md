## 1. What we collect
- **Account:** email, password (hashed), date of birth (for the 18+ check and age display), verification status.
- **Profile:** display name, tagline, bio, photos, interests, communication preferences, relationship goals, and other fields you choose to add.
- **Activity:** matches, messages, reactions, reports/blocks, and settings (e.g., theme, accessibility).
- **Technical:** IP address and basic device/log data needed to run and secure the service (e.g., rate-limiting).

## 2. How we use it
- To provide the service: create your profile, suggest matches, deliver messages.
- To keep people safe: enforce the 18+ requirement, handle reports/blocks, prevent abuse.
- To communicate: verification and essential service emails.
- To improve and secure the product.
We do **not** sell your personal data.

## 3. What others see
- Your profile (name, photos, bio, interests, etc.) is visible to other users as part of matching.
- We **do not** show read receipts, typing indicators, or "online now" status — by design.
- Your email, date of birth, and exact age inputs are not shown to other users (your age may be shown as a number).
