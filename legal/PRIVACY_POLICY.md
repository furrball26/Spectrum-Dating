# Spectrum Dating — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** [EFFECTIVE DATE]
**Data controller:** [LEGAL ENTITY NAME] ("Spectrum," "we," "us")
**Contact / privacy requests:** [PRIVACY CONTACT EMAIL]

Spectrum is a dating service built to be calm, clear, and accessible for autistic adults. This policy explains what we collect, why, how we protect it, and the choices you have. We aim to collect the minimum needed to run the Service.

## 1. Information we collect

**You provide:**
- **Account:** email address and a password (stored only as a secure bcrypt hash — we never store your plaintext password).
- **Profile:** display name, date of birth (used to compute and show age), gender and pronouns, who you're seeking, photos and their alt-text descriptions, tagline and bio, interests, relationship goal, and autism-friendly preferences you choose to share (communication style, sensory/environment preferences, prompts, context card).
- **Approximate location:** a general city/area you enter. We deliberately **strip precise postal/ZIP codes** and show only a coarse location to others.
- **Messages and interactions:** the content of messages you send to matches, reactions, likes/skips, matches, blocks, reports, and feedback you submit.
- **Photos in messages** (where that feature is enabled): images you choose to send, which are subject to moderation (see Section 6).

**Collected automatically:**
- **Technical/usage data** needed to operate the Service (e.g., authentication tokens, basic device/browser information, and logs). If you opt in to push notifications, we store the push subscription needed to deliver them.

We do **not** knowingly collect information from anyone under 18, and the Service is not directed to children.

## 2. How we use your information
- To create and operate your account and profile;
- To find and show potential mutual matches (using your preferences, interests, age/seeking filters, and approximate distance) and to deliver messages between matches;
- To provide safety features (blocking, reporting, moderation, rate limits) and to detect and prevent abuse, fraud, and prohibited content;
- To verify email addresses and, where applicable, process a "Verified" review;
- To communicate with you about the Service (e.g., verification, password resets, and, where enabled and consented, low-frequency updates);
- To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

We do **not** sell your personal information, and we do **not** use your private messages to build advertising profiles.

## 3. What others can see
- **People you have not matched with** see only a limited public profile (e.g., name, age, pronouns, tagline, coarse city, photos, interests, and general "why you're seeing them" reasons). They do **not** see your precise location, email, or your private "context card."
- **Matches** can see your fuller profile and exchange messages with you.
- By design, Spectrum does **not** show read receipts, "online/last active" status, or typing indicators, and no one is told when you view their profile.

## 4. How we share information (sub-processors)
We share data only with service providers that help us run Spectrum, under contracts limiting their use of it. These currently include:
- **Railway** — application hosting and database.
- **Cloudflare R2** — storage for profile and message images.
- **Resend** — transactional email (verification, password reset).
- **[Image-moderation/scanning vendor, if enabled]** — automated screening of uploaded images (e.g., NSFW/CSAM detection).
- **[Push provider, if applicable]** — delivery of push notifications you opt into.

We may also disclose information when required by law, to respond to legal process, to protect the safety of users or the public, or in connection with a merger or acquisition (with notice where required). **We report apparent child sexual abuse material (CSAM) to NCMEC and cooperate with law enforcement as required.**

## 5. Data retention and deletion
- We keep your information while your account is active.
- **When you delete your account** (in Settings), we delete your profile and associated personal data, **including removing your uploaded images from storage.** Certain records may be retained for a limited period where necessary — for example, backups, and **safety/moderation records** (such as reports filed by or about you) kept to protect other users and meet legal obligations.
- You can **export your data** at any time from the app.

## 6. Photos and image moderation
Images you upload may be reviewed by **automated tools and/or human moderators** to enforce our content rules and keep the community safe, and may be stored on Cloudflare R2. Prohibited images (including any content that sexualizes minors) are removed; apparent CSAM is reported to NCMEC as required by law. Images shared in messages are visible only to the relevant match and to moderators/systems as needed for safety.

## 7. Security
We protect your data with measures including encrypted passwords (bcrypt), token-based authentication with session revocation, transport encryption (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and rate limiting on sensitive actions. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information and to respond to incidents appropriately.
