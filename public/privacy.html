Privacy Policy

This is a working draft — it accurately describes how Spectrum works today, and a final version is being completed with legal counsel. If anything is unclear, ask us via Settings → Send feedback in the app.

1. What we collect

  • Account: email, password (stored only as a secure hash — never in plain text), date of birth (for the 18+ check and age display), and review status.
  • Profile: display name, tagline, bio, photos, interests, communication and sensory preferences, relationship goals, and other fields you choose to add.
  • Approximate location: a general city or area you enter. We deliberately strip precise postal/ZIP codes and only ever show a coarse location to others.
  • Activity: matches, messages, reactions, reports/blocks, and settings (theme, accessibility).
  • Technical: IP address and basic log data needed to run and secure the service (for example, rate-limiting).

2. How we use it

  • To provide the service: create your profile, suggest matches, deliver messages.
  • To keep people safe: enforce the 18+ requirement, handle reports and blocks, prevent abuse.
  • To communicate: verification and essential service emails.
  • To improve and secure the product.

We do not sell your personal data.

3. What others see

  • Your profile (name, photos, bio, interests) is visible to other members as part of matching. Some personal fields — like your free-text context card — are only shown to people you've matched with.
  • We do not show read receipts, typing indicators, or “online now” status — by design.
  • Your email and date of birth are never shown to other members (your age may be shown as a number).

4. Sharing

We share data only with the service providers that run the platform (hosting, storage, email delivery) under appropriate terms, and with authorities where required by law or to protect safety.

5. Retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time in Settings — this removes your profile, matches, conversations, messages, reactions, and related data.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict your data. You can exercise many of these directly in the app (edit profile, export your data, delete your account), or reach us via Settings → Send feedback.

7. Security

We use password hashing, encrypted transport (HTTPS), token-based authentication, and rate-limiting. No system is perfectly secure; please use a strong, unique password.

8. Children

The service is for adults 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18; if we learn we have, we delete it.

9. Changes

We'll post updates here with a new effective date and, for material changes, provide notice in the app.
